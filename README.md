# Vendor Performance Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project analyzes vendor performance by evaluating sales, purchases, profitability, inventory, and operational efficiency. The goal is to identify top-performing vendors, optimize procurement decisions, and uncover insights that can improve business performance.

## Objectives
- Analyze vendor-wise sales and purchase trends.
- Identify top and low-performing vendors.
- Measure profitability and contribution of each vendor.
- Monitor inventory and stock movement.
- Provide interactive visualizations for data-driven decision-making.

## Project Workflow
1. Data Collection and Understanding

6. Dashboard Development in Power BI
7. Business Insights and Recommendations

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Total Sales
- Total Purchase Cost
- Gross Profit
- Profit Margin (%)
- Number of Vendors
- Top Performing Vendors
- Low Performing Vendors
- Inventory Turnover
- Vendor Contribution to Total Revenue

## Dashboard Features

## Business Insights
- A small percentage of vendors contribute a significant portion of total revenue.
- Some vendors generate high sales but have low profit margins.
- Certain product categories show consistent profitability.
- Inventory turnover varies significantly across vendors, indicating optimization opportunities.

## Recommendations
- Strengthen relationships with top-performing vendors.
- Review pricing strategies for low-margin vendors.
- Optimize inventory management for slow-moving products.
- Focus procurement efforts on high-profit product categories.
